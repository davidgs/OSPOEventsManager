# User Profile

This guide covers managing your user profile and preferences in Events Manager.

## Accessing Your Profile

1. Click on your name or avatar in the header
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown menu
3. Or navigate to **Settings** ‚Üí **Profile**

## Profile Information

### Basic Information
- **Name**: Your display name (shown throughout the application)
- **Email**: Your email address (used for notifications)
- **Job Title**: Your professional title or position
- **Bio**: A brief description about yourself and your work
- **Profile Photo**: Upload a headshot or profile picture

### Updating Your Profile

1. Navigate to **Settings** ‚Üí **Profile**
2. Click **Edit** on any section you want to update
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save** to apply changes

## Language Preferences

Events Manager supports multiple languages. You can change your preferred language at any time.

### Changing Your Language

**From the Header:**
1. Click the **Globe** icon (üåê) in the header
2. Select your preferred language from the dropdown menu

**From Settings:**
1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Find the **Language** section
3. Select your preferred language from the dropdown

### Available Languages

Currently supported:
- **English (en)** - Full support

Additional languages coming soon:
- Spanish (es)
- French (fr)
- German (de)
- Japanese (ja)
- Chinese Simplified (zh-CN)

### Language Preference Storage

Your language preference is:
- **Saved to your profile** - Persists across devices and sessions
- **Stored in browser** - Used if you're not logged in
- **Automatically detected** - Uses your browser's language setting on first visit

The application will remember your language choice and apply it automatically when you log in.

### What Gets Translated

When you change languages, the following are translated:
- All user interface text (buttons, labels, menus)
- Form labels and placeholders
- Error and success messages
- Date and time formats
- Number and currency formats
- Navigation items

## Theme Preferences

You can also customize the application theme:

1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Find the **Theme** section
3. Choose from:
   - **Light** - Light color scheme
   - **Dark** - Dark color scheme (default)
   - **System** - Follows your operating system preference

## Profile Photo

### Uploading a Profile Photo

1. Navigate to **Settings** ‚Üí **Profile**
2. Click **Upload Photo** or click on your current photo
3. Select an image file (JPG, PNG, or GIF)
4. Crop or adjust if needed
5. Click **Save**

### Photo Requirements
- **File types**: JPG, PNG, or GIF
- **Maximum size**: 10 MB
- **Recommended dimensions**: 400x400 pixels or larger
- **Format**: Square images work best

Your profile photo will be displayed:
- In the header (next to your name)
- On your profile page
- In attendee lists for events
- In user management pages (for admins)

## Activity History

View your activity history to see:
- Events you've created or updated
- CFP submissions you've made
- Files you've uploaded
- Profile changes

Access your activity history from **Settings** ‚Üí **Profile** ‚Üí **Activity**.

## Privacy and Security

- Your profile information is visible to other users in the system
- Your email address is used for notifications but can be kept private
- Profile photos are publicly visible
- Contact your administrator if you need to update sensitive information

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Update Profile
- Make sure you're logged in
- Check that you have permission to edit your profile
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact your administrator if issues persist

### Language Not Changing
- Clear your browser cache
- Check that you're logged in (preferences are saved to your profile)
- Try logging out and back in
- Check browser console for errors

### Profile Photo Not Uploading
- Check file size (must be under 10 MB)
- Verify file type (JPG, PNG, or GIF only)
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different image file
