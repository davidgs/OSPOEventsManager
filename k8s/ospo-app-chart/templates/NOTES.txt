IMPORTANT CLEANUP NOTES:
------------------------
To properly uninstall this application and all its components, use the following command:

  helm uninstall {{ .Release.Name }}

If you encounter issues where resources are not being properly removed:

1. You can force delete any remaining resources with:
   kubectl delete deployment,service,job,pod,configmap,secret -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}

2. To verify all resources are removed:
   kubectl get all,job,cm,secret -l app.kubernetes.io/part-of={{ .Release.Name }}

3. For persistent resources (like PVCs), you may need to manually delete them:
   kubectl delete pvc -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}
